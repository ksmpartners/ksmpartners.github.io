## Project Management Services

KSM Technology Partners provides technical project management for complex software and integration programs in regulated industries. Our project managers speak both business and technology — accelerating alignment and reducing the rework that comes from miscommunication.

### Our Approach

We combine agile delivery principles with the rigor required in regulated environments. Our project managers are practitioners who understand architecture, testing, and compliance — not just schedules and status reports.

**Project Governance**
Establish clear decision rights, escalation paths, and change control processes that keep programs moving without bureaucratic friction.

**Agile Program Management**
Facilitate sprint planning, backlog refinement, and retrospectives. Scale agile practices across multiple teams with SAFe or custom program increments.

**Risk Management**
Proactively identify and mitigate technical, organizational, and regulatory risks before they become delivery blockers.

**Stakeholder Communication**
Maintain transparency with executive sponsors, business owners, and technical teams through structured reporting and real-time dashboards.

**Vendor Management**
Coordinate across multiple technology vendors, system integrators, and internal teams to maintain delivery accountability.

### Certifications & Methodologies

Our project managers hold PMP, CSM, and SAFe certifications and have deep experience with both waterfall (for validation-heavy regulated work) and agile delivery models.

### Engagement Models

- **Embedded PM** — We provide a dedicated project manager embedded with your team
- **PMO Advisory** — We assess, design, or mature your project management office
- **Rescue Engagements** — We step in to stabilize troubled programs and restore delivery momentum
