## Cloud Migration Services

KSM Technology Partners helps utilities and life sciences organizations move confidently to the cloud. Our team brings deep experience in planning, executing, and validating cloud migrations that meet strict regulatory and operational requirements.

### What We Do

We partner with your team to assess your current infrastructure, define a migration strategy, and execute a phased move to AWS, Azure, or GCP. Every migration is designed to preserve data integrity, maintain uptime, and satisfy compliance requirements.

**Our cloud migration process:**

1. **Assessment & Discovery** — Inventory existing workloads, dependencies, and data flows.
2. **Strategy & Roadmap** — Choose the right migration pattern (rehost, replatform, refactor) for each workload.
3. **Proof of Concept** — Validate architecture assumptions before committing to full migration.
4. **Execution** — Migrate iteratively with automated rollback capabilities.
5. **Optimization** — Right-size resources, automate scaling, and reduce cloud spend.
6. **Validation** — Verify compliance, performance, and security post-migration.

### Industries We Serve

**Utilities** — Migrate SCADA historian data, GIS systems, and operational technology (OT) workloads while maintaining grid reliability.

**Life Sciences** — Execute GxP-compliant cloud migrations with validated environments, audit trails, and regulatory documentation.

### Key Benefits

- Reduced infrastructure overhead and capital expense
- Elastic scalability for peak demand periods
- Improved disaster recovery and business continuity
- Cloud-native security and compliance controls
- Faster delivery of new capabilities
