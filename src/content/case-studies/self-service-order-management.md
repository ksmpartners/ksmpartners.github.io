## Overview

A leader in risk management and property-casualty insurance education partnered with us to deliver enhanced self-service ordering of education products over the internet. The initiative covered contact and account maintenance, catalog browsing, and shopping cart-based ordering.

## Solution

We implemented a web service layer using Spring Boot to safely externalize strategic data and services. The architecture employed a stateless design enabling horizontal scalability and loose coupling to underlying data structures, allowing enhancements without impacting consumers.

**Key capabilities:**

- Stateless web service architecture supporting horizontal scalability
- Loose coupling to underlying data structures, enabling independent enhancement
- Rich instrumentation, monitoring, and administration interface built into Spring Boot
- Self-service capabilities including account management, catalog browsing, and cart-based ordering

## Results

- More maintainable codebase
- Faster, more stable deployments
- Better overall performance
- Lower cost of future enhancements

## Ongoing Partnership

Following the initial successful website relaunch with a modern user interface, the client maintained continuous engagement with us for over three years. This included further enhancements to the web services and strategic consultation on integration initiatives, including implementing a robust single sign-on solution.
